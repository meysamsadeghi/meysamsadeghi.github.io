I am a senior staff engineer at Qualcomm working on physical AI and autonomous driving. My research interests lie at the intersection of physical AI and generative AI. Specifically I am interested in multimodal large language models, world models, and their application for end-to-end autonomous driving.

Prior to joining Qualcomm, I did my PhD in Electrical Engineering at SUTD under supervision of Chau Yuen where I received the best PhD dissertation award for my PhD thesis. I also spent a year at Linköping University as a post doc working with Erik Larsson.
