I am a senior staff engineer at [Qualcomm](https://www.qualcomm.com) working on physical AI and autonomous driving. My research interests lie at the interesetcion of physical AI and generative AI. Specefically I am interetsed in multimodal large language models, world models, and their application for end-to-end autonomous driving. 

Prior to joing Qualcomm, I did my PhD in Electrical Engineering at [SUTD](https://www.sutd.edu.sg) under supervision of [Chau Yuen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=7VLbLUMAAAAJ&hl=en) where I received the **best PhD dissertation award** for my PhD thesis. I also spent a year [Linkoping University](https://liu.se/en) as a post doc working with [Erik Larsson](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=79Lry6oAAAAJ&hl=sv).
